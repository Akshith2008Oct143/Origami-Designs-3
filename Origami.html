Intresting facts about Camel

  1. Camels ears are furry
  2. Camel can go across the sand
  3. When they find water , they will drink as much as possible

Intresting facts about Chameleon

  1. Chameleons are reptiles which are part of iguana suborder
  2. Changing colours is a important part of chameleon's communications
  3. Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches

Intresting facts about Pigeon

  1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals
  2. Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities
  3. Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities

Intresting facts about Panda

  1. Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim
  2. Pandas go from pink to white and black
  3. Pandas have carnivorous teeth, but they eat bamboo and fruit.

Intresting facts about Cicada

  1. Cicadas can survive a huge fall as babies, or nymphs
  2. Cicadas have red-orange eyes
  3. The loud whirring or buzzing sound you hear is an all-male cicada chorus

Intresting facts about Teddy Bear

  1. Teddy bears got their name from the story that Teddy Roosevelt refused to shoot a bear cub while on a hunting trip in 1902
  2. The first toy stuffed bear was created by German toymaker Margarete Steiff in 1903
  3. The Oxford English Dictionary dates the first use of the term teddy bear to 1906
